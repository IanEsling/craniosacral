Treatment Details

Treatments are held at Jane's home on Commercial St, London E1, close to Spitalfields Market.

Sessions cost £40 (£30 concessions) and last approx 1 hr.

Appointments can be made Wednesdays and Thursdays between 9am and 3pm, Fridays 4pm to 8pm and Saturdays 2pm to 5 pm.

It is a good idea to indicate the nature of the issue before the session by email or phone and I will be able to give some advice as to how many treatments may be required. However, the number of sessions a client will attend will be their decision dependant upon the benefit they are obtaining from the treatment.

Sessions are conducted fully clothed and involve lying down comfortably on a treatment bed.
